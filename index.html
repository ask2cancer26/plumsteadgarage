Our story

Est. 1986 — Serving SE London

Plumstead Garages has been looking after the vehicles of Plumstead, Charlton, Abbey Wood, Woolwich and beyond since 1986. Nearly 40 years of loyal clients — private owners, fleet operators and commercial lease companies — built by doing the job properly every time.

We're not just any garage. We're AA approved, RAC approved, RMI members, and City & Guilds and IMI certified. Those accreditations mean something — they're independently verified standards that protect you.
